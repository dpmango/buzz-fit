Basis for Calculator

Patient Retention

  • 88% of the population in the U.S. is considered to be health illiterate.
  • Based on research we identified the average panel size per full time equivalent (FTE) physician to be 1,800 patients. We use your inputs to establish an approximate panel size for your practice and calculate the number of patients that are deemed health illiterate.
  • Buzz Fit content tells patients about services available by speciality, it also reminds patients of the importance of and suggested frequency for elective tests and procedures and encourages patients to discuss these with their physicians. Best of all we do all this using everyday language, relatable actors and via digital media, a method with the highest rates of connection with the target audience.
  • Research shows that information delivered at the point of sale (i.e. just before the consultation in your case) converts to at minimum a 9.2% increase in sales across a range of service industries.
  • Applying the min 9.2% to your estimated panel and discounting by a further 60% based on the fact that 3/5 of health illiterate patients simply will not have an issue that they are ready to discuss at present, we arrive at the incremental revenue by multiplying the cost of a basic procedure by the increase in sales, which we then reduce to net margin (13%).
